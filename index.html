Click "New Point" and use the keypad to enter in a new coordinate into the table. Once at least two coordinates are entered, click "Update Graph" to plot the points and generate the Line of Best Fit, as well as the equation for the line and the data's correlation coefficient. Learning Objectives


The student will demonstrate the ability to create a table of data in order to form a scatterplot.


The student will examine and interpret a dataset's line of best fit equation and correlation coefficient.


Real World Applications


The line of best fit is a way to summarize any set of data using a single linear function. The correlation coefficient gives a measure as to how strongly the data is correlated.
